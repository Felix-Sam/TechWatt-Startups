Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

At Tech Watt, we gather personal information such as your name, email, and payment details when you interact with our platform, including purchasing AI models, projects, or signing up for courses.

We also collect usage data like your IP address and browser type to improve your experience. Cookies are used to enhance site performance and user experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

The data we collect is primarily used to provide personalized services, process payments, and improve our platform. We may also use it to communicate service updates and marketing offers.

3. Payment Information

All payments are securely processed by third-party providers such as Paystack, and we do not store your financial information.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no system is 100% secure, and we encourage users to be cautious when sharing personal information online.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. Additionally, you can opt out of marketing communications at any time.
