  • Autonomous Navigation and Path Planning
  • Building Obstacle-Avoiding Robots

Module 6: Final Project and Deployment

  • Planning Your Final Robotics Project
  • Building and Programming a Complete Robot
  • Testing and Fine-Tuning
  • Presenting Your Robotics Project
